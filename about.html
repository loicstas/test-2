Zohra Benhammou

Zohra Benhammou (1994, Brussels) is a Belgian-Moroccan filmmaker. Drawing on her background, she delves into the subtleties of personal narratives and heritage. Through her work she aims to convey the nuances of culture, religion and identity, weaving connections that bridge different realms and fostering understanding.

Romy Mana

Romy Mana (Born Romy Vanderveken, 1994, Brussels) primarily works with performance, sculpture, writing, video and film. In many cases her work combines aspects of different media, blurring boundaries between conventionally distinct categories. She works with a content that can vary from being very personal, a memory, notions of her heritage to the more universal, notions of displacement and loss, finding ways of representing through shifts, ruptures of the visual and linguistic forms of her works.
